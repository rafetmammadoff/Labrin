Why choose study abroad

More than 50 thousand Russian students receive education abroad (according to UNESCO). This figure is growing every year, as studying abroad opens prospects for a brilliant career in any field of activity.

Education in the United States , Britain , Canada and European countries is built on other principles. Abroad, students and students have more independence, responsibility, teamwork. The emphasis in education abroad is on developing leadership skills, critical thinking and social skills.

Schoolchildren and students are available the most current subjects - from robotics to diplomacy. The learning process and the excellent infrastructure of foreign schools and universities allow you to try yourself in all kinds of professions and hobbies, engage in scientific research and participate in creative projects of international class. Educational institutions abroad have a broad partner network and, in addition to education, provide great opportunities for practice and internships.

Students of foreign schools and universities receive a rich experience of intercultural communication. Study abroad allows you to expand the horizons, makes a person more open and flexible. The modern world quickly changes the rules of the game, and these qualities are necessary to feel comfortable and confident.
