About the program

The two-year course was developed by the Glion Institute in conjunction with Grenoble École de Management (GEM) and is designed for professionals who see themselves in leadership positions in hospitality and hospitality. Suitable for people with experience in this industry, and for those who are going to change their specialty. The program is accredited by the associations AMBA, AACSB, EQUIS and NEASC.

The first year:theoretical and practical classes in international marketing, audit, innovative management, corporate finance, geopolitics, negotiations, strategic and risk management and other disciplines, as well as a block of courses in hospitality management.

Requirements for admission:

  • a bachelor's degree in any specialty;
  • work experience - 3 years (full employment);
  • IELTS 6.5 (or other certifications of the language).
