About country

The Australian continent is unique: pristine nature and rich fauna coexist with skyscrapers, business quarters and prestigious universities. The education system in Australia is built according to the British model. At the same time, the cost of training in Australian universities is significantly lower than in the US and European countries (30-40%). Universities of the country occupy high positions in international educational ratings. According to one of the most famous and respected editions of the Times Higher Education, the three largest Australian universities entered the first hundred of the World University Reputation Rankings for 2016.

Prospects

Diplomas of Australian universities are highly valued all over the world. Universities with international affiliates take part in student exchange programs. In this country with a high standard of living, economic potential and the need for various specialists, a favorable migration policy operates. After completing the undergraduate program, a student can obtain a work visa for two years. A promising area is the hospitality industry, which is actively developing in Australia.
