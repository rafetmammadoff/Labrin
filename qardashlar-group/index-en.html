Qardashlar Group

We are QARDASHLIQ LLC Construction Company, established in 2003 by Mr. Famil Ismayilov. Our company has a license to operate in the field of construction, which covers the entire construction site with the approval of the license or annexes. General Director of our company Mr. Movsum Ismayilov has many years of experience in the field of construction and the new generation is contributing to the application of advanced technology.

During the 18 years of its activity, our company has implemented a number of state projects, including 24 projects of the Ministry of Education, 5 projects of the MKSIF, 5 projects of the Ministry of Agriculture, 3 projects of Hajigabul Executive Power, 1 project of Goranboy Executive Power and 1 project of the State Border Service. In total, 39 projects have been successfully completed during this period and the total cost of the projects is AZN 44,996,230.
